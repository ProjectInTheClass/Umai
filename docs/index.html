Introduction

Umai is an innovative AI-powered cooking app that transforms your culinary experience. Whether you're a beginner cook or a seasoned chef, Umai helps you discover recipes, get personalized cooking advice, and explore new cuisines with ease.

Key Features

Umai offers a range of innovative features to enhance your cooking journey:

  • AI Recipe Generator
  • Personalized Recommendations
  • Ingredient Substitution Suggestions
  • Smart Grocery Lists

From generating recipes based on ingredients you have to suggesting creative cooking techniques, Umai is your ultimate kitchen companion.

App Views

Umai app interfaces designed for intuitive user experience:

Recipe Generator

Input your available ingredients, dietary preferences, and cuisine type. Umai's AI instantly creates personalized recipes tailored just for you.

Cooking Assistant

Real-time cooking guidance with step-by-step instructions, video tutorials, and AI-powered tips to help you cook like a pro.

Additional Capabilities

Umai adapts to your preferences, supports multiple languages, and offers dark mode for comfortable browsing.
